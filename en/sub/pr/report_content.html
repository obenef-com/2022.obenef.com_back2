2018.12.05
Obenef's Smart Content Test Platform Selection and Support Results

The Ministry of Science and ICT and the National IT Industry Promotion Agency announced on the 29th that the results of testing support for companies selected for the "Smart Content Test Platform Support Project" are expanding.

This project consists of development test support field that supports customized testing and development consulting for each company and online service technology to improve the quality of products developed by domestic small and medium-sized smart content companies and release them in a timely manner.

This year, a total of 81 companies were selected and supported in 50 development test support fields and 31 development infrastructure support fields with high growth potential as global small and medium-sized companies, and since the project began in 2016, companies selected for this project have made various achievements.

Two years after its launch, Mykoon, which serves the Internet radio app "Spoon," which recorded 5 million downloads, surpassed 1 billion won in cumulative sales, and the transaction amount of the used trading app "Beongae Marketplace," which received test support, surged from 17.8 billion won in the first quarter of last year to 62.3 billion won in the third quarter of this year. In addition, Obenef, a development infrastructure support selection company, signed a memorandum of understanding (MOU) with an Indonesian buyer for export consultation of about $1Million.
